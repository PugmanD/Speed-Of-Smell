Where it all Began


Have you ever heard that very interesting sound coming from the backend of another human being? Well not to be vulgar, but that's called a fart‐which is a mixture of 59% nitrogen, 21% hydrogen, 9% carbon dioxide, 7% methane and 4% oxygen, but that is not the point. The point is have you ever wondered how long it will take the "stench" to get to you? Well for your entertainment we hired a group of scientist to figure out the actual statistics and it turned out that it is extremly diffucult question to find an answer to.
